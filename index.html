CAO

The CAO, if you want to attend any of the national universities then you better become familiar with the CAO. For many this is the main cause of stress throughout the leaving cert, it is the one variable you cannot predict. The CAO is the college gatekeeper. Fortunately previous years of CAO statistics are public information and free to everyone, We provide the previous four years of CAO stats. We hope by doing this, it will save you vital time wasted searching for this information elsewhere.

Colleges

As speciality careers on the rise, the so called real world is changing far too quickly for the established colleges to keep up, as a result many smaller colleges are offering speciality courses. We believe students should have access to at least basic information about every college no matter of size. Providing the college requires leaving cert points for entry we will provide information about the college such as location, estimated student population, and course information. ability.

Courses

What good is the CAO, if you don't know everything about the courses you are interested in? We cover every course that is currently available through the CAo, We are always expanding our library and records, we currently have more information about the courses on offer in the well established colleges as they have more information available. We do not differentiate between courses, we simply provide as much information as we can.
