Information not opinion

We have all been there, it's time to consider college, What should be an exciting time, can often become stressful. While we appreciate everyone is trying to help with college tips, course recommendations, job opportunities and of course the CAO.

We want to provide with all the information that is publicly available, in one central place. We hope that this information can help and serve as a point of reference if confused about the CAO points from previous years or about college information such as course and module details.

CAO

The CAO, if you want to attend any of the national universities then you better become familiar with the CAO. For many this is the main cause of stress throughout the leaving cert, it is the one variable you cannot predict. The CAO is the college gatekeeper. Fortunately previous years of CAO statistics are public information and free to everyone, We provide the previous four years of CAO stats. We hope by doing this, it will save you vital time wasted searching for this information elsewhere.

Every College

As speciality careers on the rise, the so called real world is changing far too quickly for the established colleges to keep up, as a result many smaller colleges are offering speciality courses. We believe students should have access to at least basic information about every college no matter of size.
